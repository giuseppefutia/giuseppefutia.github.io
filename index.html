Giuseppe Futia

Giuseppe Futia Website

Giuseppe Futia is Communication Manager and Research Fellow at the Nexa Center for Internet & Society at Polytechnic University of Turin. since February 2011. He holds a Master Degree in Media Engineering from Politecnico di Torino. Since 2008, he collaborates with the Italian newspaper “La Stampa”, especially on Internet & Society topics. Giuseppe holds data analysis and data visualization skills, useful to both sustain the outreach of some of the Nexa projects, and to support research in the field of open data.
